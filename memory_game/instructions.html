♦ Instructions ♦

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Playing is very simple - you turn over one card and then try to find a matching card. There are 20 levels to unlock as you try to score higher. Score is 1 point for two cards matched. For unmatched cards, it is recorded as 1 wrong move. To unlock a level, you need 10 points and increments of that.

Buttons Usage - What are these buttons for?

This button allows you to play the game again without resetting your score to zero. Cool, isn't it?
